# Canadian SMB Valuation Benchmarks

Reference data for valuing private Canadian SMBs, particularly in industrial/recycling sectors.

## EV/EBITDA Multiples by Sector (Private, Canadian SMB)

| Sector | Low | Mid | High | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Scrap metal / recycling | 3.0x | 4.5x | 6.5x | Higher for non-ferrous heavy, lower for pure ferrous |
| Industrial distribution | 4.0x | 5.5x | 7.5x | |
| Waste management / environmental | 5.0x | 7.0x | 10.0x | Municipal contracts command premium |
| General manufacturing | 3.5x | 5.0x | 7.0x | |
| Transportation / logistics | 3.0x | 4.5x | 6.0x | Asset-heavy discount |

**Private company discount vs. public comps**: Apply 15–25% haircut for illiquidity and size.

## Multiple Adjusters

| Factor | Adjustment | Direction |
|---|---|---|
| EBITDA < $1M | −0.5x to −1.5x | Down |
| Customer concentration >30% in one customer | −0.5x to −1.0x | Down |
| Owner-operator with no management depth | −0.5x to −1.0x | Down |
| Long-term contracts / recurring revenue | +0.5x to +1.0x | Up |
| EBITDA growth >15% p.a. (3-year) | +0.5x to +1.5x | Up |
| Real estate included | Separate value; don't capitalize in EBITDA multiple | Separate |
| Environmental liabilities | Deal-breaker or price reduction; get Phase I/II | Flag |

## Typical Deal Structures (Canadian SMB)

- **Asset purchase**: Most common for <$10M deals; buyer preference for tax step-up
- **Share purchase**: Seller preference (capital gains exemption access); negotiate reps & warranties
- **Earnout**: Common when revenue/EBITDA visibility is low; 1–3 years; 15–30% of purchase price
- **VTB (Vendor Take-Back)**: Common; 10–30% of price; 3–5 year term; prime + 1–2%
- **Senior debt**: 2.5–3.5x EBITDA; major chartered banks or BDC; 5–7 year amortization
- **Equity**: Balance of purchase price after debt and VTB

## QSBC / Capital Gains Exemption (2024/2025)

- Lifetime Capital Gains Exemption (LCGE): ~$1.25M per individual (indexed)
- Applies to share sales of Qualified Small Business Corporations (QSBC)
- Key tests: Canadian-controlled private corp, >50% of assets used in active business, holding period
- Can be multiplied across family members with proper estate freeze / family trust planning
- Note: Sellers will strongly prefer share deals to access LCGE — factor this into negotiation

## Return Thresholds (Buy-Side)

| Target | Rationale |
|---|---|
| IRR ≥ 20% | Minimum for SMB buyout |
| MOIC ≥ 2.5x (5-year hold) | Equivalent to ~20% IRR at standard leverage |
| Payback period ≤ 5 years | Sanity check on cash generation |
