## Search Guidance
1. Check most recent quarterly earnings for public comps (Sedar+ for Canadian)
2. Check AER / MERI for regulatory filings if target operates in AB/SK energy
3. Cross-reference broker CIM claims against Statistics Canada sector data
4. Use Bank of Canada Business Outlook Survey for credit conditions and capex intent
