# Commodity & Market Data Sources

Quick reference for where to pull pricing and market data.

## Scrap Metal & Steel
- **AMM (American Metal Market)** — amm.com — US scrap indices, HMS #1/2, shredded; paywalled but widely cited
- **Fastmarkets** — fastmarkets.com — Global scrap, ferrous and non-ferrous; widely used in industry
- **Steel Index / S&P Platts** — spglobal.com/commodityinsights — Ferrous benchmarks
- **ISRI (Institute of Scrap Recycling Industries)** — isri.org — Industry reports, grade specifications

## Non-Ferrous (LME)
- **LME official prices** — lme.com — Copper, aluminum, zinc, nickel, lead, tin; official settlement and forward curves
- **Kitco** — kitco.com — Free real-time and historical LME data; good for quick checks
- **InfoMine** — infomine.com — Historical commodity price charts

## Macro / Economic Data
- **Bank of Canada** — bankofcanada.ca — CAD/USD, interest rates, Canadian economic data
- **Statistics Canada** — statcan.gc.ca — Canadian industrial output, manufacturing, trade
- **US Federal Reserve (FRED)** — fred.stlouisfed.org — US macro data, industrial production, PMI
- **Trading Economics** — tradingeconomics.com — Good aggregator for global macro indicators

## Equity / Markets
- **Yahoo Finance** — finance.yahoo.com — Paul's current portfolio tracking tool
- **TMX / TSX** — tmx.com — Canadian equities, TSX listings
- **SEDAR+** — sedarplus.ca — Canadian public company filings (equivalent to EDGAR)
- **EDGAR** — sec.gov/edgar — US public company filings
- **Finviz** — finviz.com — Screening, sector heat maps, US equities

## M&A / Deal Data
- **Capital IQ** (if available) — Most comprehensive; paid
- **PitchBook** — Private deal data; paid
- **CVCA** — cvca.ca — Canadian Venture Capital & Private Equity Association; deal reports
- **MergerMarket** — Paid; good for deal flow and multiples

## Industry Reports (Free/Low Cost)
- **World Steel Association** — worldsteel.org — Global steel stats, scrap usage data
- **Bureau of International Recycling** — bir.org — Global scrap trade data
- **Natural Resources Canada** — nrcan.gc.ca — Canadian mining and minerals data
